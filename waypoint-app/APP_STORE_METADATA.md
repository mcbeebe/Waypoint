## Description (4000 chars max)
Waypoint is your personal guide through California's disability services — designed by parents, for parents.

Navigating IEPs, Regional Centers, insurance appeals, and government programs shouldn't require a law degree. Waypoint's AI Navigator understands California disability law and gives you clear, personalized, actionable guidance in the language you speak.

KEY FEATURES:

AI Navigator — Ask questions about IEP meetings, Regional Center services, insurance denials, SSI, Medi-Cal, and more. Get responses calibrated to your situation, from collaborative to assertive to adversarial tone.

Action Plan — Your personal to-do list, powered by AI. Each action comes with step-by-step instructions, scripts for what to say, and links to relevant legal resources.

Calendar & Deadlines — Never miss an IEP meeting, appeal deadline, or therapy appointment. Get alerts when important dates are approaching.

Document Vault — Keep your IEPs, evaluations, insurance letters, and medical records organized in one secure place.

Multilingual Support — Available in English, Spanish, and Vietnamese.

BUILT FOR CALIFORNIA FAMILIES:
Waypoint knows the Lanterman Act, IDEA, Section 504, Medi-Cal, CCS, and SSI. It understands all 21 Regional Centers and their processes. Whether you're just starting your journey or fighting an insurance denial, Waypoint meets you where you are.

PRIVACY FIRST:
Your family's data is encrypted and never shared. We use industry-standard security (Supabase with row-level security) and never sell your information.
