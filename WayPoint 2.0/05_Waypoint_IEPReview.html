Issues & Suggestions
Critical Goal 2, Page 5
Vague speech/language goal
"Improve communication skills" is not measurable. IDEA requires goals with specific, measurable criteria and timeframes.
Suggested revision: "By [date], Teddy will use 3–4 word spontaneous requests across 3 different settings with 80% accuracy over 5 consecutive data points, as measured by SLP data collection."
📖 See: IDEA §300.320(a)(2) — Measurable Goals
Critical Behavioral Support, Page 7
Missing behavioral baseline data
The behavior section references "occasional meltdowns" but provides no frequency, duration, or intensity data. Baselines are required for meaningful progress measurement.
Action: Request a Functional Behavior Assessment (FBA) before the next IEP meeting. The district must complete this within 60 days of consent.
📖 See: CA Ed Code §56521.2 — Behavioral Interventions
Critical Services, Page 9
Speech services below typical range
IEP lists 30 min/week of speech therapy. For a child with ASD + SLI, typical range is 60–120 min/week. Current hours may be insufficient.
Action: Request increase to minimum 60 min/week. Ask the SLP for data supporting the current frequency, or request an independent evaluation.
📖 See: IDEA §300.320(a)(4) — Services to Advance Goals
Suggestion Accommodations, Page 10
Consider adding sensory accommodations
No sensory accommodations listed despite ASD diagnosis. Common accommodations include: sensory breaks, noise-canceling headphones, flexible seating, and reduced visual clutter.
Info Transition, Page 11

Teddy is currently 6 — no transition planning is needed yet. Waypoint will remind you when this becomes relevant.
